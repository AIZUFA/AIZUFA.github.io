About Lahore Campus

Where Tradition Meets Innovation in Lahore

Bahria University Lahore Campus (BULC), located at 47-C, Civic Center, Johar Town, Lahore, is a premier academic institution that blends Bahria University's tradition of excellence with the intellectual and cultural richness of Pakistan's cultural capital.

The campus offers a stimulating environment for learning with modern classrooms, dedicated research labs, a well-stocked library, and a vibrant student life that includes sports, cultural events, and professional development activities.

  • Strategically located in Johar Town, Lahore
  • Modern infrastructure and academic facilities
  • Strong placement and career support services
  • Active student councils and cultural societies
  • Transport facility for students across Lahore
